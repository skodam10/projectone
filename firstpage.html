Lebron James

LeBron James will start at point guard for the Los Angeles Lakers next season, per Yahoo Sports, a head-scratching announcement for a player who has often served as the de facto floor general for his team since entering the league in 2003.

It is also not the first time he has been slotted in at point guard. In 2017, James recorded a triple-double as Cleveland point guard and dropped 34 points with 13 assists in a different game against the Chicago Bulls in the same role.
